What we do

Small changes make a big difference!

Sometimes the words we use on our websites can make it hard for you to find and understand our services on Canada.ca. Federal departments are working with us to make it easier!

Project goals Project targets Outcomes for Canadians
Findability: top task information is easy to find on Canada.ca A 20% incease in average task findability, or a findability rate of 80% Canadians spend less time looking for information on Canada.ca
Completion: top task information is easy to understand on Canada.ca A 20% incease in average task Completion, or a completion rate of 80% Canadians understand the information they find on Canada.ca
